About Me

Hi! I am Avinash Pandey, a 26-year-old guy who is enthusiastic about software and mathematics.

I like designing systems and have experience with C++, Python, Golang, and JS. Algorithmic problems are my brain-sharpening tool, and sway + Notion is my productivity tool (check my dotfiles). Sometimes, I contribute to open-source projects.

Although entire mathematics is fascinating, my interest lies primarily in graph theory, combinatorics, and number theory. Olympiad-style problems keep me engaged sometimes.

Here are some of the open-source stuff I have built.

  • D3 Graph Theory - Interactive and visual graph theory tutorials made using d3.js.
  • Omnibang - A Chrome/Firefox extension for searching websites directly from the address bar.
  • Simple Graph Playground - Create your graph diagrams, you know, just for fun.
